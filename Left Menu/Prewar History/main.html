The societal history of Terane before the Demon War can be itself split in roughly four more ways; there is a history before the Great Demons (of unknown duration), a history after the Great Demons but before the Republic (a few hundred years), a history after the Republic but before the formation of modernist spearmanship (a few dozen years), and the remaining margin before the Demon War (ten-ish years). A great deal of scholarship exists for that last chunk of time, a similar but smaller amount for the one before it, a pittance for the one before that, and nothing but legends for antedemonic Terane.

In other words, pre-Republican times are taught in centuries, pre-modernist times in decades, pre-war times in years—and pre-demon times with one timeless, shapeless brush. Of those, the most immediately useful information for helping to understand Splinters/SQARLATTI (i.e. information most likely to have stuck in the average citizen’s mind, and information I’ve spent the most time developing and integrating into the game) lies in the yearly play-by-play of the time between modernism and the Demon War.
