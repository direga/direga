The societal history of Terane before the Demon War can be itself split in roughly four more ways; there is a history before the Great Demons (of unknown duration), a history after the Great Demons but before the Republic (a few hundred years), a history after the Republic but before the formation of modernist spearmanship (a few dozen years), and the remaining margin before the Demon War (ten-ish years). A great deal of scholarship exists for that last chunk of time, a smaller amount for the one before it, a pittance for the one before that, and nothing but legends for antedemonic Terane.
